Heart Festival

Course: Visual Communication II, Fall 2023, Website

Heart Festival is a conceptualized, mock benefit event that aims to raise funds for the San Francisco Arts Education Project in order to uplift Bay Area children who partake in the arts. It is a two day music festival featuring all local and Bay Area native artists. For this event, I created a website consisting of all the information needed for an attendee. Assets made for the site include a logo and two merchandise items.
